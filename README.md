---

Ghostty is an offline-first journal and todo app built for privacy. All your data is encrypted with AES-256 and stored only on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking.

### Features

- Rich text journal with photos and videos
- Todo list with home screen widget
- Biometric authentication
- Screenshot and screen recording prevention
- Dark and light themes

### Security

Everything is encrypted before it touches storage. Your thoughts never leave your device.

### Note

This app works completely offline. A small amount of mobile data may be required only when downloading app updates.
